dear prague,

you're a fresh one in the collection - my first home abroad, and I couldn't wish for a better one. though I've only gotten to know you for three months, life there felt like a huge net of coincidences; every single person I met was somehow intertwined in this net, and I was constantly followed by the feeling that I was exactly where I’m supposed to be. I think I left a part of my heart there, you keep crossing my mind and I can’t help but marinade in nostalgia every once in a while.

some of the highlights were blind portraits of strangers, navigating tourists in my first week, bohemia exhibition in kunsthalle, kino aero, citrus overdose, holešovická tržnice, italian-turkish-slovenian christmas dinner and secret santa, pro-palestine protests, slovenian wine on an empty stomach, kolache baking workshop, artic bakehouse cherry bombs, the inside tour of the photobooth in kasárna karlín, bohemia film awards, cafedu meetings, jzp yoga classes, tram rides, hangouts in vyšehrad and the jar of invasive questions.

you offered me a new perspective on life, introduced me to lovely people and awoke feelings in me that I’ve never been faced with before. thank you and see you again sometime!

maribor • ljubljana • vienna • prague • budapest • istria • amsterdam

beti trstenjak, march 2024
