dear istria,

whether it was the slovenian part (portorož, koper, izola, piran) or croatian part (usually umag, sometimes accompanied by road trips to other cities such as pula), you were always my family's go-to vacation destination. the specific destination varied, but the one constant of every year was driving to koper to enjoy pizza in planet tuš at least once on the trip - the pizzeria has since been closed down, which was a heartbreaking experience.

however, I’ve come to realize that I lack the inner peace to properly enjoy the nothingness of summer vacations, so it has always been somewhat nightmarish (especially around the 8th grade mark, combined with adolescent insecurities). it’s sad to think of it now - I may have failed to fully appreciate this time with my family that I’ll never get back again and I instead spent that time being angry at the world.

thank you and see you again sometime.
