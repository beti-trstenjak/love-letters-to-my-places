dear amsterdam,

I only got to know you for a week one summer with my family, but that one week was a memorable one. the highlights range from accidentally walking into the red light district to visiting museums (stedlijk, rijksmuseum, eye film, van gogh) to drinking sinesappelsop (orange juice), visiting a pride parade, sitting on museumplein, buying tony’s chocolonely chololates and finding ajvar in the supermarket. evenings were usually spent taking ferry rides and drinking fizzy lemonade in eye film museum cafe.

another memorable thing was westergas market - becoming best friends with the guy at the vinyl stand (this friendship was so genuine that he ended up showing us his bike and the interaction ended with giving him a maribor postcard).

that’s it for now, dank je & see you again sometime!

maribor • ljubljana • vienna • prague • budapest • istria • amsterdam
